1. Introduction

This Policy explains how Artisans237 collects, uses, stores, and protects information about Customers, Providers, and visitors.

2. Information We Collect

Account info (name, phone, email, hashed password, role), location (town/quarter), Provider trade category and trial/subscription status, MTN MoMo transaction references (not your PIN), and basic usage data.

3. How We Use It

To run your account and dashboard, match Customers with nearby Providers, process manual payments and trial periods, verify your email, detect fraud, and improve the Platform.

4. How We Share It

Between matched Customers and Providers (contact + location, only after a request is accepted), with payment-related communication where relevant, or if required by Cameroonian law. We do not sell your personal data.

5. Storage and Security

Data is stored in a SQLite database on our hosting provider. Passwords are hashed; sessions use JWTs. No system is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active, and as needed afterward for legal/accounting purposes. You may request deletion, subject to legal record-keeping limits.

7. Your Rights

You may request a copy of your data, request correction, request deletion, or withdraw consent for non-essential communications — contact [support email].

8. Children's Privacy

Artisans237 is not intended for use by individuals under 18.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

Used to keep you logged in and remember your language preference. If Artisans237 runs advertising (for example, via Meta/Facebook Pixel), an additional cookie may be used to measure which ads led to a visit and to help show relevant ads to people likely to be interested in our services. This Policy will be updated with the specific tool's name before it goes live, and you can decline non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time.

10. Changes

Material changes will be announced on the Platform.

11. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of the Republic of Cameroon, including applicable cybersecurity/cybercrime provisions.
